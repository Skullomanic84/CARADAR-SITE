About us

Caradar is a provider of security technology solutions for the automotive, transport and other industry verticals, our solutions are backed by latest advances in smart sensing, telecommunications and cloud computing. We have solutions for GPS tracking, fuel level monitoring, fleet management, cold chain monitoring. We serve customers in insurance, banking, telecommunication, transportation, logistics, manufacturing, construction among others.

Our Mission

To connect our customers to what they care about through modern and reliable assets monitoring and surveillance technologies

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of sensing, tracking and monitoring products and solutions powered by latest advances in mobile technologies, data analytics, cloud computing and the internet of things (IoTs)

Our Values

We believe in ethical, fair and transparent business. We retain customers by providing quality products & best services.
